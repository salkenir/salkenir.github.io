How to sleep better

Good sleep is vital to a healthy lifestyle. Do you find yourself waking up feeling tired, exhausted, and restless? If you say yes, you are not alone. These issues affect many people all over the world.

Tips to create a restful environment for sleep

  • Temperature: experts agree that the sweet spot for temperature whilst sleeping is between 60 and 67 degrees. Personally, sleeping in a warm room results in a restless night.
  • Lighting: It is reccomended to sleep in a dark setting. This means no TV or outside lights while you sleep.
  • Peace and quiet: Quietness is essential to a good nights sleep. However, if you are like me and need some sort of white noise to sleep, there are many soothing options online to fall asleep to. Even things around the house like a fan can help create a calm but still ambient environment for sleep.
  • De-clutter your bedroom I have stressed how important environment is when it comes to sleep, but I have not mentioned cleanliness. When your surroundings are cluttered, your life can get even more stressful. Sleeping in a messy room just creates an environment for restlessness. So, clean up your space!
