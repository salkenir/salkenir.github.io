How to reduce anxiety

Anxiety is something everyone has experienced in some form or level. There are every day anxieties, then there is clinical anxiety that impacts people's daily lives.Anxiety does not have to be something that takes over our lives, and there are ways to reduce it.

  • Deep breaths: When we are anxious, our breathing becomes rapid and shallow. Grounding yourself by taking some deep breaths in through your nose and out through your mouth can help reduce anxiety. Try this breathing exercise:
    Inhale deeply for a count of 4. Hold your breath for a count of 4. Exhale for a count of 4. Repeat several times.
  • Get outside: Exercise is one of the best anxiety remedies, immediately and long term. Going for a walk creates a diversion from worries and releases muscle tension..
  • Get distracted: Try anything that gets your mind somewhere else. Try watching a show or movie, listening to music, or calling a friend.
  • Healthy diet: What we eat and put into our bodies has a direct impact on our mood. It is important to eat a lot of fruits and veggies to promote a healthy inside, thats your mind AND your body. Science shows that vitamins and minerals actually may help to reduce anxieties.
