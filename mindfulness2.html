How long does it take to form a habit?

If you are trying to practice mindfulness in your day-to-day life, you may be wondering how to make minfulness a habit. So, how long does it take to form a habit?

"It can take anywhere from 18 to 254 days for a person to form a new habit and an average of 66 days for a new behavior to become automatic."
