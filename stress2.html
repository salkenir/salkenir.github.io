How to relieve stress

Stress is a natural part of human life. Work, school, and relationships all add stress onto our lives. While we cannot change everything that happens around us, we can learn to manage and deal with the stress so it does not negatively impact our lives.

  • Open dialouge: It is really important to talk to other people about how you feel. Simply venting yout feelings to someone you trust can take weight off your shoulders.
  • Laugh more: It seems silly to say that simply laughing can help relieve your stress, but it is true. When you laugh and feel happiness your body has an emotional response that lets off endorphines in your brain, making you feel happy. Watch a funnny movie, call a friend, or scroll on Tiktok when you are feeling extra stressed in the moment.
  • Get enough sleep: Lack of sleep adds on to your stress, making everything worse. You need energy to even deal with the stress, so imagine all that piling up and also not getting a good nights sleep.
  • Journaling: Keeping a journal helps to get your thoughts out and onto paper. Keep a journal to write in when you are feeling happpy, sad, or stressed. The journal helps you to be able to look back and track how far you have came, or those days that seemed like you would never see the end of, realizing you actually made it through.
